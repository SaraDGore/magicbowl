Rice with Black Beans, Sweet Potatoes, and Pico de Gallo

Rice
  • 1 cup white rice
  • 2 tsp salt

Cook the rice.

Black beans
  • 1 cup black beans
  • 2 tsp salt

Cook the beans.

Sweet potatoes
  • 1 cup white rice
  • 2 tsp salt

Cook the sweet potatoes.

Pico de Gallo
  • 1 1/2 c chopped tomato
  • 1/2 cup white onion, diced
  • 1 tsp garlic, minced
  • 1 tsp jalapeno, seeded and diced
  • 1/2 c chopped cilantro (or parsley)
  • 2 tbsp lime juice

Combine tomatoes, onion, garlic, and jalapenos to taste. Add cilantro (or parsley if using). Squeeze in lime juice. Season with salt and pepper. Tastes better if you let the flavors mingle for a while before serving.
